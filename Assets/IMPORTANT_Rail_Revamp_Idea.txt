IMPORTANT — Rail Building System Revamp Idea
=============================================
Date: 4 April 2026

Revamp the entire rail building system to NOT be based on drawing splines.

Instead, model it like real model train sets where you have discrete physical
track pieces that snap together:

- STRAIGHT piece
- CURVED piece (fixed radius arc segments)
- JUNCTION piece (3 exits — like a turnout/switch)
- Possibly other specialty pieces per scenario

Each piece is a self-contained unit with defined connection points/sockets.
Any piece can connect to any other piece at matching sockets.

This is more realistic to how model trains actually work — you buy a box of
track pieces and click them together, rather than drawing freeform curves.

Different scenarios could have different sets of available pieces.
